Account Setup Guide

1

CREATING READ ONLY POLICY

  • Log in to your Amazon Web Services Management Console
  • Select Identity and Access Management (IAM) under the Security & Identity section
  • Select Users from the Dashboard menu on the left panel
  • Select Create New Users
  • Enter a unique username for your new user
    Make sure to check the box next to Generate an access key for each user and then click Create at the bottom right of the page
  • You should receive a confirmation message, verifying that your user has been created
    You can choose to copy the credentials directly from this page under Show Users Security Credentials or Download Credentials and keep them in a safe place. Select close after you have saved your credentials
  • Click on your newly created user from the user selection menu
  • Select the Permissions tab. Then click Attach Policy under the Managed Policies section.
  • Search for the ReadOnlyAccess policy option and select that as your policy. Click Attach Policy when you are finished.
2

OBTAINING YOUR DETAILED BILLING REPORTS

  • Return to your home page on the Amazon Console
  • Select S3: Scalable Storage in the Cloud under the Storage & Content Delivery section
  • Select Create Bucket
  • Name your S3 bucket such that you will know to designate your billing reports to be sent to it
  • Open the Billing and Cost Management console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/billing/home#
  • Select Preferences from the Dashboard menu on the left panel
  • Select the Receive Billing Reports check box and enter the name of the Amazon S3 bucket you created in Phase 2: Steps 1-4
  • Click on the highlighted sample policy link in the note directly under the verify button and copy the sample policy that is generated for your S3 bucket
  • Return to the S3 bucket that you previously created in Phase 2
  • Select the Properties button on the top right corner of your S3 bucket's page
  • Expand the Permissions section
  • Select the Add bucket policy button and paste the sample policy that was generated for your S3 bucket in Phase 2: Step 8 Click Save to finalize your permission edits for your S3 bucket.
  • Return to the billing reports page from Step 7 and verify the S3 bucket that you wish to send your billing reports to.
  • After your S3 bucket has been verified, under Report, select the check boxes for the reports that you want to receive.
  • Select Save preferences and begin receiving your detailed billing reports :)
    **Note: It can take up to 24 hours for AWS to start delivering reports to your S3 bucket. After delivery starts, AWS updates the detailed report files multiple times per day.
3

SETTING UP YOUR TRACKIT ACCOUNT

  • Sign up for a TrackIt.IO account by visiting http://trackit.io/#/signup
  • Once you create your TrackIt.IO account, you will have to allow TrackIt.IO to retrieve your billing reports from AWS
  • Select Add new key
  • Enter the key credentials you generated in Phase 1
  • Click Submit to save your key credentials
    You can get your IAM User Policy by clicking on Generate Policy
    Congratulations! You have successfully created and configured your TrackIt.IO account.
4

DISCOVER TRACKIT.IO

  • Once AWS starts generating detailed reports and sending them to your designated Amazon S3 bucket, TrackIt.IO will access those reports and give you insights to your AWS account usage and cost.
